About Us

N.C.A Apartment

Welcome to Next Camp Apartment! Our apartments is spacious and attractive, with plenty of room for you to make yourself at home.

We offer a variety of amenities to make your stay as comfortable as possible, including:
Parking space: You’ll never have to worry about finding a parking spot with our convenient on-site parking.
Fast internet: Stay connected with our high-speed internet, perfect for streaming, browsing, and working from home.
Garden: Enjoy the beauty of nature with our lush garden, perfect for relaxing or taking a stroll.
In-house kitchen: Cook up a storm in our fully-equipped in-house kitchen, complete with all the appliances and utensils you need.
3 bedrooms: Our apartments come with 3 spacious bedrooms, perfect for families or roommates. Toilet: Our apartments come with a clean and modern toilet.
Furniture made from Muvura hardwood: Our furniture is made from high-quality Muvura hardwood, ensuring durability and comfort.

Our apartment complex is located in the Kanombe neighborhood, just a short distance from Kanombe International Airport. The neighborhood is known for its peaceful atmosphere and proximity to a variety of attractions, including restaurants, shopping centers, and public transportation.

We offer a variety of leasing options to fit your needs, including lease terms, rent prices, and move-in specials. We hope you’ll consider making Next Camp Apartment your new home! If you have any questions or would like to schedule a tour, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
